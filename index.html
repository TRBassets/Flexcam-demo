FlexCam – Intelligent Camera Tracking for Blender

FlexCam enhances Blender's built-in camera system with smooth, lifelike camera motion. It intelligently tracks, follows, and adjusts to your subjects automatically, providing seamless, cinematic results with minimal effort.

Real-Time Tracking and Following

FlexCam dynamically adjusts your camera view in real-time, following subjects accurately as they move, ensuring you never miss a crucial moment.

Stationary and Follow Modes

FlexCam can switch between stationary mode for smooth panning or dynamic follow mode for active tracking.

Multi-Camera Setups

Easily deploy multiple FlexCams in your scene to capture every critical angle.

Dynamic Field of View

Automatically adjust the camera’s FOV in real-time to perfectly frame your subjects, accommodating their movements, sizes, and the cinematic needs of each scene.

Multi-Subject Tracking

FlexCam tracks multiple subjects simultaneously, making it ideal for dialogue scenes, fight sequences, and complex interactions.

Follow Curve

FlexCam smoothly tracks along curve paths while dynamically adjusting the camera’s field of view (FOV) and subject tracking.

Rapid Prototyping and Experimentation

Quickly create and refine dynamic camera shots. FlexCam enables fast setup and immediate experimentation, helping you discover the perfect angles and movements for your scenes in seconds.

Why Choose FlexCam?

Traditional camera systems require extensive planning and setup, often hindering spontaneous creativity. FlexCam changes this by operating in real-time, instantly responding to subject movements and scene adjustments. Simply reposition your camera or shift your subjects—FlexCam intelligently adapts, empowering you to create captivating and dynamic cinematic shots effortlessly.
